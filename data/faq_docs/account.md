# Account FAQ

**How do I reset my password?**
Click "Forgot password" on the login page and enter your account email. You'll
receive a reset link valid for 1 hour. If you don't see the email, check your spam
folder before requesting a new one.

**How do I update my email address?**
Go to Account Settings and update your email under "Contact Information." You'll
need to verify the new address before it becomes active.

***How do i close my account?**
Contact support and request account closure. Note that closing your account 
permanently deletes your order history and any active subscription will be
cancelled without a prorated refund for the remaining period.

**How do I update my preferred contact method?**
Let support know whether you'd prefer email, phone, or chat for follow-ups, and
we'll save that preference to your profile so future conversations respect it.
