# Shipping FAQ

**How long does shipping take?**
Standard shipping takes 5-7 business days within the continental US. Expedited 
shipping takes 2-3 business days. International orders take 10-15 business days
depending on customs processing.

**How do I track my order?**
Once your order ships, you'll receive an email with a tracking number and a link
to the carrier's tracking page. You can also see the latest status by asking us
for your order ID.

**What do I do if my package is lost?**
If your tracking shows no movement for more than 5 business days, contact support
with your order ID and we will open an investigation with the carrier and send a
replacement or refund if the package cannot be located with in 10 business days.

*Do you ship internationally?*
Yes, we ship to most countries. International orders may be subject to customs
fess and import duties, which are the customer's responsibility and are not
included in the order total.
