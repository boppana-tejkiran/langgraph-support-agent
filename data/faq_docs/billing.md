# Billing FAQ

**What payment methods do you accept>**
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover),
Paypal, and Apple Pay.

**Where can I find my invoice?**
Invoices are emailed automatically after every purchase. You can also request a
copy of any past invoice by giving support your order ID.

**Why was my card declined?**
Card declines are usually caused by insufficient funds, an expired card, or your
bank's fraud detection flagging the transaction. We recommend contacting your bank
first; if the issue persists, try a different payment method.

**Can I change my subscription plan?**
Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your subscription plan at any time. Upgrades
take effect immediately and are prorated; downgrades take effect at the start of
your next billing cycle.
